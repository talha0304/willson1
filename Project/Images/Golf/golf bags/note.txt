1.FootJoy Deluxe Boston Bag use glossy leather material, modern, less fouling and easy to clean with a soft cloth. Especially this fabric also has superior water resistance, so that the travel bag will keep its beauty and original as new.

Deluxe Boston Bag can be carried on the hand or worn on the shoulder, at the discretion of the user, in order to enhance the physique and fashion trend for the discerning.
